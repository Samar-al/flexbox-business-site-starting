Local Products

Where do we buy our products ?

Because what we use and where does it come from is as important as the final product itself, we will share with you the stories of the products we use on our dishes

All of our products are sourced locally.

They come from farmers within the region. Our products are organic and follow the rules of permaculture. Our meet is also organic. We use meet, from time to time, for some of our dishes, so you won't be able to find meet everyday in our menues.

We like to make simple recipes that makes the products stand out!

We like to experiment and make you discover new tastes and introduce you to new associations!
